All sessions must be paid in advance before a meeting time is scheduled.

The cost is €35 per session (approximately $40 USD). Each session lasts approximately 60 minutes.

Scheduling

Sessions are scheduled individually after payment is received and take place online via Zoom.

You may cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment and receive a full refund.

Refunds are issued only when cancellation occurs more than 24 hours before the scheduled session.

Coaching services are available only to individuals 18 years of age or older.

Clients worldwide are welcome. Clients are responsible for attending according to their own local time zone.

Confidentiality
